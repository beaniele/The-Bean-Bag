My friend (re)introduced me to Cookie Run Kingdom about a year ago, right when the 'Song of the Night Sea' update was taking place. I had an account on the Pure Vanilla server a couple years back, but lost it due to my adolescence and lack of understanding for the game. Given my comeback, I told myself I'd actually take it upon myself to pay attention to the story and understand the whole storyline of the game. And boy, have I not regretted doing so. I remember trying to indulge further into crk's lore, when I stumbled across a patch note leak talking about Shadow Milk cookie's future release. Honestly don't know what was so alluring about him that instantly made me want to pull him from his gacha banner during his release period, but as I scrounged his given backstory by DEVSIS, the parenting company of the cookie run franchise. Simply put, I resonated with the majority of his personality and character build, and it's safe to say that ever since my discovery of him, he's been my favorite. The game overall is extremely enjoyable. Towards the end of each day, I unwind by playing for an hour or so. And whenever I'm doing anything passive, such as cleaning my room, coding, or anything else that doesn't really require much thought, I listen to the 'Cutscene Theatre' episodes provided by crk's youtube team - basically the storyline without game play interruption. Peak fiction, if you ask me, both the gameplay, and the storyline, and omg don't get me started on the art. However, that, is a later conversation for the future.
